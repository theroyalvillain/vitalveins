Donation Guidelines

✓ Important Requirements:

  • You must be between 18-65 years old
  • Minimum weight: 50 kg
  • Wait 40 hours after alcohol consumption
  • Wait 48 hours after taking antibiotics
  • Do not donate on an empty stomach - eat a healthy meal
  • Drink plenty of water - stay hydrated
  • Wait 90 days between donations

✓ Before Donating:

  • Have a nutritious meal 2-3 hours before
  • Avoid fatty foods - eat fruits, vegetables, and iron-rich foods
  • Drink water or juice before donating
  • Get adequate sleep the night before
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing

✓ After Donating:

  • Rest for at least 10-15 minutes
  • Have a snack and drink fluids immediately
  • Avoid strenuous activity for 24 hours
  • Increase iron intake over next few days
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol for at least 24 hours
